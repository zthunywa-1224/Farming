Farm Animal FAQ

Cows are ruminants and need a steady supply of roughage (hay or pasture). Typically they eat 2–3 % of their body weight in dry matter each day, split into 2–3 feedings.

Regular hoof trimming, deworming every 6–8 weeks, and providing clean water and shelter from extreme weather are key. A balanced mineral supplement helps prevent deficiencies.
